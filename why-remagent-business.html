Why Businesses Choose Remagent

The Difference

Built Specifically for Contact Center Teams

Every feature on Remagent was designed around one problem: finding the right remote contact center professional, fast.

🎯
You Search. You Choose. You Invite.

You're not waiting for applications to come in. You define exactly what you need, browse professionals who match, and send invitations directly. The hiring decision stays with you — always.

✅
Every Professional is Pre-Screened by Humans

No bots, no keyword matching; we aggressively filter out bot and high volume application apps. Real contact center experience is verified before a profile goes live. When you search Remagent, you're only seeing people who have actually done the job.

💰
No Agency Fees. No Commissions. Ever.

Staffing agencies charge 15–30% of a hire's salary. Remagent charges none of that. You find the talent, you hire directly, and you pay the professional — not a middleman.

🔍
Filters That Actually Mean Something

Filter by platform (Zendesk, Salesforce, Genesys), channel (Voice, Chat, Email), language, availability, pay range, industry background, and more. Every filter maps to real, verified profile data.

⚡
From Search to Shortlist in Minutes

Set your filters, see matching professionals instantly, review their full profiles and send invitations in a single workflow. No back-and-forth.

🔄
Scale Up or Down Without the Overhead

Need two agents for a seasonal push? Ten for a new product launch? Remagent's talent pool is always available, without long-term staffing commitments.
