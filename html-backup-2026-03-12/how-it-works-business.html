From Search to Hire in Hours

Four steps from sign-up to your first qualified match — and you're in control the entire way.

The Process

Simple by Design. Powerful in Practice.

Remagent is designed to get you to qualified candidates fast — without the back-and-forth, the middlemen, or the wasted hours.

1
Create Your Company Profile

Tell us about your business and review the Remagent services agreement. This enables you to search our database of Remagent Employment Professionals and initiate offers.

→ Takes about 10 minutes to complete
→ Company profiles are verified before going live
→ Professionals see your company info when you invite them
2
Post a Position

Describe the role you're filling — the support channel (voice, chat, email), the platforms used, the schedule, the pay range, and a few other attributes. Your position post becomes the filter that surfaces the right professionals.

→ Make posting public or private
→ Each position generates its own filtered candidate pool
→ Private postings are only seen by candidates you invite
3
Search, Filter & Invite

Browse the professionals who match your position. Review their full profiles — verified experience, platforms, languages, availability, and pay expectations — then send invitations directly to the people you want to talk to.

→ Every result is a pre-screened, qualified professional
→ No unqualified resumes to sort through
→ Invite one — or several — to create your shortlist
4
Connect & Hire

When a professional accepts your invitation, you connect directly through the platform. No agency, no middleman. You conduct your own interview and make a contracting or hiring decision.

→ Direct connection — you own the relationship
→ Manage the number of candidates that apply
→ Adjust filters and find more matches just as quickly

Common Questions

Questions We Hear Most

How does Remagent ensure quality Call Center personnel?

Remagent senior managers personally interview each professional candidate. All our managers possess years of experience in the call center industry — including Operations, Customer Service, telephony, and more. We look for experience, communication skills, and professional presence — how they communicate, carry themselves, and represent a brand.

How are professionals screened before their profiles go live?

Every professional is reviewed by a member of the Remagent team — not an algorithm. We verify their contact center experience, confirm the platforms they've listed, and ensure the profile accurately reflects their background. Only vetted professionals appear in your search results.

What does it cost?

There are no placement fees, no agency commissions, and no per-hire charges. You pay the professional directly — Remagent is not involved in compensation. Subscription details are available on the pricing page.

What if the professional I hire doesn't work out?

You can search and invite again just as quickly. Because the talent pool is always available and the filter system is instant, finding your next match takes no more effort than the first one did. There's no starting over from scratch.

Can I post multiple positions at the same time?

Yes. You can post as many positions as you need simultaneously. Each position generates its own filtered candidate pool based on the requirements you set. Managing multiple roles is built into the platform from the start.

How long does it take before I see matching professionals?

Typically within minutes of posting a position. The filter system matches your requirements against existing professional profiles in real time. If your requirements are very specific, Remagent will also notify you when new matching professionals join the platform.
